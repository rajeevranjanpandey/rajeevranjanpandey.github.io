// 01 — Profile

About Me

I work on system-level modeling and optimization of large-scale energy networks, with emphasis on decentralized coordination, EV charging, and cyber-physical systems.

My research integrates mathematical optimization, metaheuristic design, and structured AI to build decision-reliable frameworks for real-world infrastructure.

Focused on physically consistent modeling, scalable algorithms, and causal reasoning in systems where correctness is non-negotiable.
